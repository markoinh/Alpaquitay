Who We Are

A Community United
by Peruvian Culture

Alpaquitay Tasmania is an independent, community-based Peruvian folklore dance group based in Hobart, Tasmania. We celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Peru through the living traditions of Andean, Coastal, and Amazonian dance and music.

Our name honours the alpaca — a symbol of the Andean highlands — and our spirit: warm, resilient, and deeply rooted in community. Since our founding, we have shared the colour and joy of Peruvian folklore with thousands of Tasmanians across festivals, schools, and cultural events.

Performances

Bringing the Andes
to Every Stage

From intimate community events to large festival stages, our ensemble of trained dancers and musicians delivers authentic, high-energy Peruvian folklore performances. We perform dances from every region of Peru — Marinera, Huayno, Festejo, Tondero, and more — in stunning traditional costumes.
